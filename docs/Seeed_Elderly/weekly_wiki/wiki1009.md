### [SenseCAP S2107](https://wiki.seeedstudio.com/SenseCAP_S2107/)

SenseCAP LoRaWAN¬Æ S2107 temperature Sensor measures temperature at the range of -50¬∞C~300¬∞C. Benefiting from LoRa and IP66 design, this sensor features stability and reliability and can cover a long transmission range while keeping lower power consumption. Direct contact temperature is an important indicator in many scenarios, especially in liquid detection, food monitoring, storage cold chain, industrial boilers, aquaculture solutions.
